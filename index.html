What is slipping — and why?
Critical · Technical
GFC drawings still pending — 18+ months past planned completion
Planned completion was Oct 2024. Recurring delays from design consultant (IHRD, GH, Canteen, Polytechnic, ITI/KIHM, CSA/Auditorium buildings). Multiple scope changes (canteen kitchen layout, structural column revisions) required revised drawings, compounding the delay. Phase remains open.
Critical · Schedule
Inspection & testing not yet mobilised — window opens in 8 days
Planned start 14 May 2026. With construction completion targeted 23 May 2026, there is a critical overlap risk — inspection cannot commence if construction site is not handed over.
High · Financial
₹39.9 L outstanding across RAB 04 & RAB 05 with no collection date set
RAB 04 (₹19.7 L, due Mar 2026) and RAB 05 (₹20.1 L, due Apr 2026) are both sent but uncollected. RAB 05 is under bill scrutiny. Combined outstanding = 50.9% of all billed value.
High · Scope
External utility / master plan revisions caused downstream delays
Conflicts in MEP services, stormwater alignments and hydropneumatic system changes needed TS committee approval. Resolved but contributed to timeline extension across construction phase.
